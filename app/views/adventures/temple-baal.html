2. The temple of Baal

Rumours and hooks

Wo Gong, the mine boss of Brynderwold was running an smuggling operation, taking profits out of the mine. He found a amulet one day in the mine and kept it. Unbeknowst to him, this amulet was a token of Baal and allowed the servents of the God of Pestilance to whisper to his subconsious.

Not long after he discovered a hidden chamber with a huge shrine to Baal, ancient God of decay and pestilance within the mine. This was not a coincedence. The whispering told him he was going to be discovered and told him he needed a distraction. He followed a ritual that he thought would cause a distraction to help him escape. This ritual brought a plague to the town and terrified Wong. It was at this point the adventurers appeared, cleared up the plague and destroyed the idol. Wong was arrested and put in jail alongwith his associate Karg.

This adventure starts with the adventurers relaxing at the 'Half Stump' when in walks Sergeant Kaden looking doer. He informs the players that Wong has vanished and the guardsmen stationed to his cell has been brutally murdered.

2. The ambush (combat)

The lanscape starts to change as you ride into the fog of the morning. The open plains start to change to a rock and tor covered landscape and the landscape becomes much more wet and wild.

Soon you are walking through a canyon, hills changeing to boulder covered slopes, changing to cliffs raising on both sides. The weather becomes foggy and wet and soon your cloths are soaked through. The horses become agitated and nervous.

2 days in.

5 cultists wait in ambush. Armed with daggers. On one of the bodies - a key with a circle + maggot motif. Check to see if they recognise it from Wong's sketches. Also another has a piece of paper , very used with the phrase 'Connect the letters of your master'

Drop rock from top of boulder. perc check or dex dodge

Dartmoor style surroundings

3a. quicksand (trap)

As you start the morning of your third day riding your surroundings start to change again. Gone are the rocks and cliffs. Shrubs around you get larger and more tangled throughout the day. The ground becomes increasingly wet and muddy and trees, misshapen and nobbled like old men appear all around you. The air becomes wet and humid and biting insects com thick and fast. You are now on the outskirts of a very dank and foreboding swamp. The dry paths either continue to the straight into the swamp or circle around to the North.

Taking path into swamp -

The air is thick with fog and insects of various sizes. Even the walkable land beneath your feet sinks with each step. The smell of decay fills your nostrals. Ahead of you through the fog you make out a large shape . As you draw in you see what appears to be a skeleton of a huge beast half submerged in the mud. Its the skeleton of a dragon, long decayed. However on the underside you can see some scales still intact, the incredible resilience lasting even now...

Quicksand - Dragon sinks after scales removed. luck (Dex) save to not sink. 5 squares to get out. Strength check to move.

Giant leech attack if feel like it needs more.

North path takes much longer and loses more food.

4. Hag (NPC interaction)

In the distance through the reeds and haze you can see a tree of tremendous width, 30 foot wide at its largest. Long dead, it appears to be hollow and the warped and knarled bark opens at the side to make a kind of doorway. A feeble light emenates from within.

A weak, raspy and female voice beckons you nearer.

Ixna the hag wants to use the adventurers to track down and kill a plant golem she raised. It was supposed to be her slave but the spell didn't quite work and it escaped. It is semi intelligent and just wants to be free.

She offers to guide the group to the temple if they return with proof that it is dead. She is more then happy to send them deeper into the swamp as it will be easier to catch and eat them later on

5. Temple

As you continue deeper into the murky swamp, you come to a clearing. Mostly dry land opens up ahead, devoid of trees and shrubs. Nothing at all is growing here and in the centre is the a spire of an ancient temple. It has sunk over the possibly thoousands of years it has been here so it now stand crooked. The at the bottom of the structure is a doorway, the area that would be underground now has been recently cleared of dirt.

The structure is covered with carvings of flies, maggots and demons festering in stages of deacy and corruption. The air is still and no insects bother you here.

Room 1 (Puzzle)

As you approach the door you see definate signs of recent activity in the mud outside the door. Even though the metal door is showing signs of age it looks very sterdy and intact. What strikes you as odd is that there is no handle. Instead a 5 by 5 set of squares is inset into the centre of the door and within each one a keyhole. Surrounding this is a circle of strange glyphs...

Entrance has a lock. Runes to read, then puzzle to complete. Success will mean the door opens silently. Using wrong lock means a mechanical alarm is set off. Door can be broke down with strength (athletics) test. This however will cause a lot of noise

Room 2 (combat)

The temple is dark inside and the air is wet and damp. A smell of decay and putrifaction is heavy in the air. The stone walls glisten with moisture and slime from unknown sources. On the floor strange insects and small creatures scurry away from your, hiding in the dark corners.

This entrence room is 10 foot square and empty. A single exit leads deeper into the temple, the solid wooden door lying on the floor. Above the exit a carving sof Baal looks down at you from a pile of dead things.

If the group entered silently they can catch 2 cultist guards in room 2. Otherwise 4 guards are ready for them and will have advantage.

Room 3

A large crack, a foot in width runs across the centre making the room at two different inclines. A lot of rubble has fallen in from the ceiling but otherwise the room is empty save the increasing amount of insects, and larva crawling across the floor. Also strange misshapen rats, flop around in the corners, struggling to walk.

Empty. Two exits

Room 4 (trap + combat)

As you enter this large chamber you immediately feel the air catch in your throat. This might have been a dedication room at one point, digusting frescoes can just about be seen on the walls. However that time has long past as its been completely overtaken by fungus and mould. Thick carpets of yellow slime coat large patches of the walls and ceilings and mushrooms of all size and shapes are everywhere. Large yellow ball fungus are distrubuted around the floor, a slow mist of spores eminating from them

On the far side of the floor lies one of the Baal cultists, his body burst apart with yellow sporing fungus.

Fungus has taken over. Step carefully to avoid spore bomb. Mushroom men. Dead cultist 4 mushroom men

Dex check each round. FAiling hits ball fungus and spores explode. Everyone make luck (con) save lose 1 con per round. Con lose is cumalative

Room 5 (treasure)

This small room appears to be empty apart from a lot of rubble and dirt from the partly collapsed ceiling.

If alarm tripped then another 3 cultists are waiting in ambush here. Otherwise a perc check will notice a glinting within the rubble. Searching will cause a dex check. Failing check cases a cave in. Dex to dodge. This triggers alarm.

Solid gold water bowl for dedications. Worth 300gp

Room 6 (trap)

Opening the wooden door reveals a large chamber, very tall and wide. Most of the floor is taken up with a square pool. The pool looks undamaged and is filled with what might have been water at one point but is now a dark brown, fetid sludge , slowly oozing and bubbling.

You assume this must have at one point been an annointing chamber to purify the priests before going deeper into the temple but how the worshippers of Baal purified themslefves is something you do no want to think about.

Larvae and maggots are crawing out of the pool all around the edge. The room exit is on the other side of this nightmare and it looks like the only way out is through...

plague worms in pool. Adventurers have to find a way across. Wooden doors or rope or trees outside. falling in water gives worm fungus

Room 7 (combat)

This large chamber is the main hall of the temple. It is huge, 100 foot across. At the far end is a huge recongnisable statue of Baal. It mimics the one from the mine but twice as large. Below it the area is lit from a glowing orb at the end of a high priets staff. He seems to be preciding over a ritual of some kind. Wong is on a raised plinth infront of the priest, being held down by two cultists. His mouth has been filled with mud and the priest has a blade held high.

The hall looks very unstable as cracks run up and down the walls and across the ceiling.

High priest. If alarm hasn't been set the players have the element of surprise. 7 cultists in total. so whats happened indictaes how many left in room.
